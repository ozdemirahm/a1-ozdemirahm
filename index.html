There is three important steps you need to follow in a good quality of camping.

  • Plan
  • Required Tools

Plan

First,you need to make a plan for time,place and weather.

Time

You choose when you go to camp and how many days to stay there.

Place

You need to choose somewhere allowed to camping and the opportunity of this place's nearby should fit your plans.

Weather

It is about both time and place so you need to follow weather conditions in your camping area and make preparations according to this weather events.
